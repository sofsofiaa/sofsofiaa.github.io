Chrome Matcha

Chrome Matcha blends metallic textures with soothing green hues, evoking the calm and energy of a matcha latte. Here, a real photo of an iced matcha latte was used. Generative Ai,Runway,Adobe and procreate was used to create the look of this new piece.

Soap Swirls

An experimental project inspired by texture, movement, and color play. I designed a series of visuals that mimic the fluid, organic patterns of soap swirling in water, translating everyday beauty into a creative brand aesthetic. This project let me explore abstract design, layered color palettes, and the balance between chaos and harmony in visual storytelling.
