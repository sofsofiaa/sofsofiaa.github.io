Chrome Matcha

Taking inspirtaion from the generative artist, Arttsu Koskela creates in a generative art format, created by a digital photo, then manipulated with coding. As I take that inspiration, I created Chrome Matcha, a digital art piece that captures the essence of a refreshing iced matcha latte with a modern twist manipulated with adobe photoshop, procreate, and runway inserted a similar formulacreated by artist: noise, saturation, glare, color added sharpen, chrome abrasion, perspective blur to imitate effects.
